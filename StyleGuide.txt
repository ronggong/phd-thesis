A basic style guide for the document to maintain consistency

* Document sizes
Paper size (B5): 250x176 mm
Text size: 189x125 mm

* Fonts
Text font size: 12 pt, 
Text font: Times New Roman (default)
Algorithms font: TeleType (\texttt)
Dataset font: Times New Roman
Syllable names: cousine

* Figures
Size: **x125 mm
Font size for labels and axis labels: 12 pt, Times New Roman default
Labels - \label{fig:<figName>}

* Equations
Labels - \label{eqn:<eqnName>}

* Tables
As minimal lines as possible
Labels - \label{tab:<tabName>}

* Chapters
Labels - \label{chap:<chapterName>}

* Sections
Labels - \label{sec:<secName>}

* Sub-sections
Labels - \label{subsec:<subsecName>}

* References
apacite
Labels - \label{name:year<a/b>:stub}

* Writing style
 - Indian, Turkish and Chinese terms - Italicize once, transliterate to Roman from next occurrence onwards.
